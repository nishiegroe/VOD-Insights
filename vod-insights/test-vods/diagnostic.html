⚠️ Diagnostic Instructions

  1. Open DevTools: Press F12
  2. Go to Performance tab
  3. Click Record (red circle)
  4. Click ▶ Play Video below
  5. Wait 10 seconds (video will record performance data)
  6. Stop the recording in DevTools
  7. Analyze: Look for Main thread tasks, JS execution, rendering
  8. Go to Network tab
  9. Reload page and play video again
  10. Check: chunk sizes, timings, bandwidth
  11. Go to Console and click buttons above for detailed metrics
